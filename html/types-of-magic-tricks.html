Types of Magic Tricks & Illusions

Definition of Magic Tricks & Illusions

"Magic tricks" or "effects", or "illusions" are illusions of impossible, or supernatural accomplishments which are created by using purely natural means, in order to entertain an audience [1]. So what is the difference between a "magic trick" and an "illusion"? The answer to this question, is that there is no difference because everything that you see during a magician's performance is not real and thus it is an illusion.

As entertainment, illusionists use psychological deceptions and manipulation methods to perform accomplishments that are apparently in violation of natural law [1]. Sleight of hand and complicated secret mechanical devices help convince witnesses that they have just observed the magician play out the unimaginable [1].

Conclusion

Finally, magic tricks is the expression of the art of magic, which is performed by thousands of professionals around the world. Magic art, is based on the hidden human desire to believe in the existence of the supernatural / unreal. We can say that magic tricks in the form of Transformation, are safe to perform and entertaining to watch. However, magic tricks like "Sawing a woman in half", "The Sword Cabinet" or most of the "Escaping" tricks are very dangerous. Despite that, magic tricks are a fascinating experience for adults, and an unforgettable experience for the children.
