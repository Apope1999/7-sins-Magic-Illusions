Restoration Magic Tricks

A woman is sawn into two separate parts and then magically rejoined. A card is torn in fourths and then restored piece by piece to a normal state. Another frequently used restoration magic trick is the "rubber band restoration". The magician snaps a rubber band in front of the audience, and after rubbing his arms together, he magically shows up a fully restored rubber band. "Restoration" magic tricks, put something back into the state it once was [1].
