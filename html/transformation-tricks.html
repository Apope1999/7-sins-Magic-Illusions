Transformation Magic Tricks

A magician requests a volunteer to "pick a card, any card" from a deck [1]. The magician then shows the card to the volunteer and asks "is this your card?" It is not the right card, but the magician tells the volunteer, "here, hold it for a second," handing them the card and then picking card after card from the deck, none of which is the card the volunteer picked. The magician asks, "will you look at that first card again?" whereupon the volunteer finds the card in their hand to have magically become their card [1]. Another well-known transformation magic trick, is "spoon bending". It was first performed by Israeli magician Uri Geller, in the 1970's in live American Television and since then it has been strongly connected with Geller's name. This trick is performed with the magician holding a silver spoon and then magically slowly bending it.

Another transformation magic trick is Card Transformation trick. In this trick, the magician shuffles the deck, pulls out four random cards, shows them to the audience and then puts them back in on top of the deck. Finally, he pulls out the first four cards of the top of the deck, and they magically appear to be four Kings / Aces.
