Penetration Tricks

Penetration tricks focus on objects such as coins, phones etc. passing through other objects. Many famous magicians have performed this live in front of a live audience.

In this video we see a less known magician that goes by the name "Benno Six" who performs penetration tricks in LAX Venice Beach. He uses magic to make coins and a phone go through a bottle. In the end there is a twist. He makes the bottle go through actual living people.
