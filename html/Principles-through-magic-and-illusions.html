Principles through magics and illusions

And what is magic, if not a mystery that sparks the cold fire of impossibility and faith in humanity. Reawakening humbleness from the unexplainable stimuli we cant control, is the art of illusions, and through inflicted selfknowledge we recognize our lack of understanding and there motivation and persistance to know, flows.

But how do we show these subtle principles of magics and illusions through magic tricks? Magicians have been carrying out informal behavioural experiments on people for centuries and have built up a huge array of techniques – many psychological – to create their mind-bending effect but the truth is that you can use these tools to make points, add interaction and make your speech and ideas memorable[2].

In the following show the magician is using a simple trick to ignite faith in young people and motivate them into helping and get help from others. His show will keep you interested in his speech while later on you may never forget how interconnected humans are.
