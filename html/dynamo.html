Dynamo

Dynamo, borned with the name of Steven Frame, in Bradford, England to an English mother and a Pakistani father. He suffers from Crohn's Disease, which made him small in stature for his whole life. During his childhood he was frequently bullied and for that reason his grandfather taught him his first trick which made him look as though he was very heavy so that other children could not pick him up.

His first appearance on media was with the radio documentary called "Estate of Mind". In 2011 his dream of a TV Series became a reality when the first series of "Dynamo: Magician Impossible". During this show, Dynamo performed an exciting stunt: he walked across the River Thames. After that feat, tickets and awards have been coming very fast.

On 5 July 2012, Dynamo was promoted by the magicians' society, The Magic Circle, to Associate of the Inner Magic Circle with Silver Star for Performance. Also Dynamo won the "Magician of the Year" award from The Academy of Magical Arts 48th Annual Awards Show.
